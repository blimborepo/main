The all new /co/ series Blimbo and His Friend (previously The Fagtitiously Retarded Fagntics of Blimbo and His Friend) follows the misadventures of the titular Blimbo and His Friend as they team-up with random, often dysfunctional, collaborators to attempt to produce a successful comic/cartoon, all the while navigating the day-to-day encounters with their eccentric roommate couple Doodle and Glimbo as well as the absurd world they find themselves in. The show satirizes many of the typical tropes of animated television along with many of the prevalent archetypes inhabiting/orbiting its industry, primarily those of interest/notoriety to /co/.

The series is set in an over-industrialised metropolis, VilleVille, made up of physically impossible haphazard architecture and inhabited by an equally chaotic random assortment of unique creatures. This eclectic population of cartoon characters both represents and features the various mindsets and stylings, respectively, ubiquitous to /co/ and the broader cartoon community the show is satirizing, a few of which will cross paths and collaborate with Blimbo. The majority of the city's citizens, including our main cast, reside in a near infinitely tall and wide apartment complex, the Happy Day Tower (previously the Babel Tower 8), that spralls from every end of the city, weaving between businesses, resturants and parks, to meet at a centrals mother building. The building is collectively owned by a triopoly of omnipotent competing entertainment conglomerates, The Fun James Sea Company, The Happy Robert Mountain company and The Good Michael Sun Company, who each engage in never-ending back and forth trade of segments of the complex amongst one another to further each's own convoluted plot to nickle and dime their way to soul ownership.

Blimbo is the neurotic and creative protagonist of Blimbo and His Friend.. She is an aspiring cartoonist, but regularly finds herself wildly off track from achieving her goals due to her impatient and erratic nature, acting partially as a surrogate for her passionate but largely unproductive board of origin /co/ . At her best, she is adventurous, resourceful and motivated when engrossed in one of her and His Friend's artistic endevours, but often that same productive driving force leads her awry once the novelty of a project wares off or a self destructive impulse tempts her. Her generally impetuous personality severely undermines any interpersonal skills she might possess, as she can't help but blurt out exactly what's on her mind or what would be most immediately satisfying to tell someone.

A contributor to these personality faults is Blimbo's deep-seated insecurity surrounding her femininity. Despite being in her early 20s, Blimbo's physical appearance most resembles a pudgy child, with boyish hippo-like features decorating her bulbous face.
